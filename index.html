Frequently Asked Questions

What is GameUp11?

GameUp11 is an online sports network that connects players with premium local venues. Through our mobile app, you can easily discover and instantly book slots for Box Cricket, Football, Tennis, Pickleball, Badminton, Snooker, and more.

How do I book a turf or court?

All venue discoveries, slot selections, and bookings are handled securely through the GameUp11 mobile app. Simply download the app from the Play Store or App Store, select your sport, pick an open time slot at a venue near you, and confirm your booking.

I own a sports venue. How can I list it on GameUp11?

Partnering with us helps you digitize your bookings and connect with thousands of active players. Reach out directly via our contact form or call our support team to get your arena listed on our Elite Network.
